What is Year Up?

Year Up: A National Movement

Year Up is a non-profit organization that trains students through an 11 month program that focuses on soft skills and hard skills necessary in the Tech Industry. Year Up is different from other organizations because of its mission; to close the opportunity divide. Year Up provides young adults from urban communities the tools and resources to enter the professional world.

Year Up Bay Area

Year Up Bay Area (YUBA) is a branch of Year Up that has two locations in the Bay Area; one in Downtown San Francisco, and another in Silicon Valley.

Learning and Development Phase

L&D stands for Learning and Development. This is the first phase of the Year Up cycle. This is a five month long phase that focuses on Technology, Business Communications, Pro-Skills and Track Related Classes. The L&D phase is split into 3 seven week modules. The final module is heavily focused on a specific technical track that each individual student can be part of. The tracks offered at Year Up Bay Area include IT, Networking, Quality Assurance, Cyber Security, and Project Coordination.
